### Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use this website and any connected Pinterest or Meta app integrations.

### Information Collected

Information that may be collected includes:

- Basic analytics and usage data (for example, page visits and browser details).
- Information you submit directly (for example, messages or feedback).
- Data provided through Pinterest or Meta APIs only when required for app functionality and permissions you approve.

### How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

- Operate and improve the site and connected app features.
- Diagnose errors, security issues, and performance problems.
- Respond to support or contact requests.
- Meet platform policy or legal requirements.

### Data Sharing

Information is not sold.

Data may be shared only with:

- Service providers required to run the site or integrations.
- Pinterest or Meta, when app actions explicitly require those platform APIs.
- Legal authorities when required by law.

### Data Retention

Data is kept only as long as needed for operational, security, and legal purposes, then deleted or anonymized where practical.

### Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

You can also control platform-connected permissions directly in your Pinterest and Meta account settings.

### Data Deletion Requests (Meta and Pinterest)

If you want your data deleted from this site or connected app integrations, use one of these methods:

- Remove this app's permissions in your Pinterest or Meta account settings.
- Contact the site owner through [About](/about/) with your request details.

### Cookies and Tracking

This site may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, performance, and core functionality.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

### Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The `modified` date above reflects the latest revision.
